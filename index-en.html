ü§î Frequently Asked Questions about Digital Addiction Test

How long does the digital addiction test take?

The test takes about 3-5 minutes and consists of 20 scientifically validated questions to assess your relationship with technology.

What is nomophobia?

Nomophobia (no-mobile-phone phobia) is the irrational fear of being without a cell phone or internet connection. It affects about 66% of the adult population.

How to cure smartphone addiction?

Smartphone addiction can be managed with: gradual digital detox, setting time limits, disabling non-essential notifications, and alternative offline activities.

What are the symptoms of digital addiction?

Symptoms include: compulsive phone checking, anxiety when disconnected, sleep disorders, concentration difficulties, and social isolation.
